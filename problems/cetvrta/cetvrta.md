Mirko needs to choose four points in the plane so that they
    form a rectangle with sides parallel to the axes. He has
    already chosen three points and is confident that he hasn’t
    made a mistake, but is having trouble locating the last point.
    Help him.
Input
Each of the three points already chosen will be given on a
    separate line. All coordinates will be integers between
    **1** and **1000**.
Output
Output the coordinates of the fourth vertex of the
    rectangle.


Sample Input 1
Sample Output 1




5 5
5 7
7 5




7 7






Sample Input 2
Sample Output 2




30 20
10 10
10 20




30 10
