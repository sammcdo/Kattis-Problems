Given two integers **A**
    and **B**, compute their
    greatest common divisor.
Input
The first and only line contains the integers **A** and **B** (**1
    \le A, B \le 10^9**).
Output
Output a single integer – the greatest common divisor of
    **A** and **B**.


Sample Input 1
Sample Output 1



5 7



1






Sample Input 2
Sample Output 2



10 15



5
